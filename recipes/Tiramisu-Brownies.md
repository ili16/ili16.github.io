# Tiramisu Brownies

## Ingredients
- **200 g** dark chocolate (70% cocoa), chopped
- **150 g** unsalted butter
- **250 g** granulated sugar
- **3 large** eggs
- **1 tsp** vanilla extract
- **90 g** all-purpose flour
- **50 g** unsweetened cocoa powder
- **1/2 tsp** salt
- **100 ml** strong brewed coffee, cooled
- **250 g** mascarpone cheese
- **50 g** powdered sugar
- **2 tbsp** coffee liqueur (optional)
- **Cacao powder** for dusting

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the Brownie Base:**
   - Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a 20x20 cm baking pan. Line it with parchment paper, leaving some overhang to make removal easier.
   - In a double boiler or microwave, melt the dark chocolate and butter together until smooth. Stir occasionally to combine.
   - In a mixing bowl, whisk the granulated sugar into the melted chocolate mixture until fully incorporated. Allow it to cool slightly.
   - Add the eggs, one at a time, whisking well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract until smooth.
   - Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gently fold until just combined, being careful not to overmix.
   - Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread evenly. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Let the brownies cool completely in the pan.

2. **Prepare the Tiramisu Layer:**
   - In a separate bowl, whisk together the mascarpone cheese, powdered sugar, and coffee liqueur (if using) until smooth and creamy. 
   - Once the brownies are completely cool, brush the surface with the cooled brewed coffee.
   - Spread the mascarpone mixture evenly over the brownies. 
   - Cover the brownies with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to set.
   - Before serving, dust the top with cacao powder. Cut into squares and enjoy your Tiramisu Brownies!
